The tile numbering system follows the XYZ (Slippy Map) standard, which is the universal way web maps (like Google Maps or MapLibre) handle layers.

Breakdown of tile/day_069/4/2/5.png
day_069: The Time Snapshot. This is the specific Day of Year (March 10th in this case).
4 ($z$): The Zoom Level.
0 is the whole world.
4 is roughly the size of the Continental US.
10-12 is regional/city detail.
2 ($x$): The Column. How many 256px squares from the left (west) the tile is.
5 ($y$): The Row. How many 256px squares from the top (north) the tile is.
